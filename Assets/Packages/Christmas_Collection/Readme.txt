Version 1.0

A beautiful package of 3D Christmas decoration. You will find:
-Two Christmas Tree (One of them with TWO different materials)
-Five Christmas Presents (With FIVE different materials each = 20 combinations)
-One Snowman
-Two Christmas Stocking (One of them with TWO different materials)


There are some prefabs that have different materials you can choose from.
All you have to do is find the prefab folder, place the the prefab in your scene and from the
material folder inside the prefab folder, choose a different material. Click the material, drag
it across the screen and drop it over the prefab.

Pretty easy!
